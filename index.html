How deals earn a spot here

I run every deal through 10 separate checks before it can earn a spot: no favourites, no paid placements. If the data isn't there or the numbers don't hold up, it doesn't get listed. A few of the big ones:

Fair size for the typeA "2-bedroom" under 1,000 sqft is a hard sell later. Size is checked against the unit type, not the brochure adjectives.
Real living spaceTotal area can hide a huge terrace. We look at what share of the sqft you actually live in.
Usable outdoor spaceBalconies add value in Dubai, but only in the right proportion to the unit.
Layouts that make senseDoes the floor plan make logical sense? Room ratios flag awkward layouts before you ever book a viewing.
